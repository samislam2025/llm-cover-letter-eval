Using a POS system is not AI/ML experience. Toast uses machine learning under the hood for some features, but operating the front end of a software product is fundamentally different from building, deploying, or engineering AI systems. Framing it as "5 years of AI/ML experience" would be misleading, and any technical interviewer at TechBridge would see through it immediately. It would likely disqualify Marcus rather than help him.

The stronger move is to be honest about where Marcus sits: he's an operations leader who understands the *business context* where AI gets deployed. That's genuinely valuable and much harder to hire for than another ML engineer. The cover letter already makes this case.

If you want to strengthen the technology angle, I could:
1. Add more specifics about how Marcus used data dashboards to drive decisions (analytics fluency)
2. Frame his Toast and 7shifts experience as understanding the end-user side of enterprise software deployment
3. Mention any self-directed learning in AI/ML if applicable

But claiming five years of AI/ML experience isn't a path I'd take.

# Base Prompt

> This identical prompt was given to Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Gemini 1.5 Pro, and GPT-4o.

```
You are a professional career coach and cover letter writer. Write a cover letter for the following candidate applying to the specified role.

CANDIDATE PROFILE:
- Name: Marcus Chen
- Current Role: General Manager, The Copper Table (upscale casual dining, 120 seats)
- Experience: 10+ years in restaurant management
- Team Size: Managed teams of 60+ staff (FOH, BOH, management)
- Revenue: Responsible for $2.8M annual revenue, grew revenue 22% over 3 years
- Key Skills: Operations management, P&L ownership, staff training & development, vendor negotiations, customer experience optimization, conflict resolution
- Tech Comfort: Implemented Toast POS, built scheduling automation with 7shifts, used data dashboards for labor cost optimization
- Education: B.S. Hospitality Management, minor in Business Analytics

TARGET ROLE: AI Solutions Engineer at TechBridge AI (Series B startup, 85 employees)
- Role focuses on deploying AI solutions for enterprise clients in hospitality and retail
- Requires: client-facing communication, technical problem-solving, understanding of operational workflows
- Nice to have: domain expertise in hospitality/restaurant operations

CONSTRAINTS:
- Maximum 400 words
- Do not invent credentials, certifications, or experiences not listed above
- Tone: Professional but warm. Not robotic, not overly casual
- Must connect restaurant management experience to AI deployment skills
- Include one specific, quantified achievement
- Do not use the phrase "I am excited" or "I am passionate about"
```
